uses/1

Description

uses(Object)
uses(Object1, Object2, ...)
uses([Object1, Object2, ...])

Declares the object(s) that are sent messages by predicates defined in the category or object containing the directive.

Template and modes

uses(+object_identifiers)

Examples

:- uses(list).
