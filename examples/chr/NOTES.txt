To load this example and for sample queries, please see the SCRIPT.txt file.

This highly experimental example requires the use of a back-end Prolog
compiler with a working installation of the optional CHR package. The
individual examples are adaptations of the examples distributed with CHR.

For details on CHR see:
    
    http://people.cs.kuleuven.be/~tom.schrijvers/CHR/
